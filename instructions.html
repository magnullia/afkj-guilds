Member Instructions

Member Instructions

Find your name in the member list (they are sorted alphabetically).

  • If you are already in the correct guild, you don't need to do anything.
  • If you are in the incorrect guild, leave it. This starts a 30-minute cooldown, after which you are able to join the new guild.
  • It's okay if you don't immediately get in. There will be some delays as slots open and close. Just be patient.

Leader/Vice-Leader Instructions

  • Please wait to leave your guild until everyone else has completed moving. We need your help to accept applications.
  • If you see a pending application, reference the member list. If it looks right, accept it; otherwise, just leave it for a main leader to figure out.
  • Please direct any questions to the main leaders. They are the most knowledgeable about this.

Feel free to reach out with any questions! We want this to go as smoothly as possible.
